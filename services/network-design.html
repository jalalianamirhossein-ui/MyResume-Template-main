Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the network design?

The design includes complete network architecture, IP planning, VLAN configuration, routing/switching design, firewall policies, and comprehensive documentation with topology diagrams.

How long does the project take?

The complete project typically takes 14-22 days, including discovery, design, build, testing, and handover phases.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we include 3 months of remote support and 30 days warranty for bug fixes. Extended support packages are available.

What equipment brands do you support?

We specialize in Cisco and MikroTik equipment, but can work with other brands including Juniper, Fortinet, and HP/Aruba.

Is the price fixed or can it change?

The base price is fixed for standard network designs. Complex requirements or additional features may require custom pricing.
